# maxplayer

##### Stop your screen saver interrupting your mplayer sessions

   maxplayer  (){ while :; do xte 'mousermove -4 20'; sleep 1s; xte 'mousermove  4 -20'; sleep 2m; done& mplayer -fs "$1"; fg; }
